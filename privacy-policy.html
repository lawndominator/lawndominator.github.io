Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Summary: Lawn Dominators uses a limited set of data and device permissions to provide lawn care recommendations, weather-based tools, photo logging, notifications, in-app purchases, and ads in the free version. We do not sell your personal information.

About Lawn Dominators

Lawn Dominators is a lawn care app for homeowners. It helps users manage their lawns with personalized schedules, diagnostics, calculators, weather-based timing tools, product recommendations, and optional premium features.

Information We Collect and Use

Location Data

We may request access to your location to support app features such as:

  • Hardiness zone resolution
  • Weather-based recommendations
  • Soil temperature lookups
  • Growing Degree Day (GDD) tracking
  • Spray condition checks

Location is used only to support these features. We do not continuously track your location in the background.

Photos and Camera

If you choose to use the photo log, the app may request access to your camera and photo library so you can take lawn photos, select existing photos, and save progress images.

Application Logs and App Preferences

The app stores information you enter — such as your lawn profile, grass type, zone, application logs, progress photos, and preferences. This information is stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may send local reminders related to lawn care timing, pre-emergent windows, GDD tracking, and application reminders.

Purchases

If you choose to purchase premium features, in-app purchases are processed through Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

Advertising

The free version of the app may display ads using Google AdMob. The app is configured to request non-personalized ads by default. Ad providers may still process limited device or contextual data as part of ad delivery, fraud prevention, and measurement.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on third-party services to function. Depending on the features you use, these may include:

  • Weather and geocoding providers for forecasts, soil temperature, and location-based recommendations
  • Google AdMob for ads in the free version
  • RevenueCat and Apple for in-app purchase management
  • Barcode or product lookup services when barcode scanning is used

These providers may process data as needed to deliver their services. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use the app.
  • We do not intentionally collect more information than is needed to operate the app's features.

Data Retention

Most user-entered data and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you remove the app or clear app data, that information may be deleted. Data handled by third-party services may be retained according to those providers' own policies.

Children's Privacy

Lawn Dominators is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke location, camera, photo library, and notification permissions in your device settings.
  • You can remove the app at any time.
  • You can contact us with privacy questions using the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
