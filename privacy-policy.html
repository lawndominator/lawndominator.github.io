Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Lawn Dominators ("we," "our," or "us") built the Lawn Dominators app as a free app supported by advertising. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. The app stores all lawn care data (application logs, schedules, photos, settings) locally on your device.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device location to provide weather data and growing degree day calculations relevant to your area. Location data is sent to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to retrieve weather forecasts. We do not store or share your location data.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Camera and Photo Library

If you use the photo log feature, the app requests access to your camera or photo library. Photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo — weather and soil temperature data (open-meteo.com)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (policies.google.com/privacy)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@lawndominators.com.
