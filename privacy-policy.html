Privacy

How Lawn Dominators handles data.

Lawn Dominators uses a limited set of device permissions and third-party services to power weather-based tools, lawn logging, photos, reminders, purchases, and ads in the free version. We do not sell your personal information.

Last updated April 18, 2026
Support support@lawndominators.com
Quick read Most user-entered data stays on your device. The app may use location, camera, notifications, weather providers, ads, and purchase services only when those features are enabled or in use.

What the app stores

Device-stored lawn data

Lawn Dominators stores your lawn profile, grass type, zone, logs, progress photos, premium status, and feature preferences on your device. Some features also use external services to fetch weather or other reference data.

Location

Weather and timing tools

If you allow location access, the app may use your coordinates to estimate hardiness zone, local weather, soil temperature, spray conditions, and similar location-based recommendations. This data is used to power app features and is not required for every screen.

Photos and logs

Progress tracking

Photos you add and application logs you save are used to document lawn history inside the app. If you intentionally enable any shared publishing feature, selected data may also be sent to the configured backend for display to other users.

Ads and purchases

Free and premium access

Free builds may show ads. Premium purchase status may be checked through the configured purchase provider so paid access can be restored across installs or devices.

Third-party services

Services used to run features

The app may rely on third-party services for weather, purchases, diagnostics, geocoding, barcode lookups, and similar features. Their systems and policies apply when those services are used.

  • Weather and geocoding providers for forecasts and location-based recommendations
  • Google AdMob for ads in the free version
  • RevenueCat and Apple for in-app purchase handling
  • Other lookup providers when specific features call them

Your choices

Permissions and contact

You can deny or revoke location, camera, photo library, and notification permissions in your device settings. You can also remove the app at any time. Questions about privacy can be sent to support@lawndominators.com.

Additional notes

Retention, children, and updates

Most user-entered data is retained locally on your device until you remove the app or clear device data. Lawn Dominators is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. We may update this policy from time to time, and the date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
